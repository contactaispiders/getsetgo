About SET(Spiders Entrance Test)

  • As we know, this is tough situation which we all are going through, hence SET program is incubated to support the STUDENT’S COMMUNITY;
  • SET is a social cause initiation taken by AiSpiders (Unit of Qspiders & Jspiders) to empower & help every needy economically backward student who wish to start his/her career in IT
  • This program is ONLY for highly serious, disciplined & focused learners. (Hence we conduct test for each section of training)
  • AiSpiders provides a solid learning platform for every aspirant to kick start your career by up-skilling with necessary industry skills in the area of AI, Data Science, Java, Python and Full stack development
  • We never give you false promise of job or 100% job guarantee. We only help you by giving complete in-depth training along with interview opportunities
  • Excludes- [This program is NOT for]
    Students who are
    • Not focused and serious about learning
    • Not decided about IT field
    • Who are trying for Gate and govt jobs
